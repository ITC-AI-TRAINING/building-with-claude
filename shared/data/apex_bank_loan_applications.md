# Apex Bank — Raw Loan Application Texts

Five raw applicant submission texts for Lab 3 extraction exercise.
Students will extract structured `LoanApplicationRecord` fields from each.

## Application 1

Rajesh Kumar here, I want to apply for a home loan. I am looking at a property
worth around 55 lakhs in Pune. My monthly salary is 90,000 rupees and I have
been working at the same company for 6 years. I would like to repay over 20
years. My credit score was 742 when I checked last month. Please let me know
the next steps.
